Quick Reference

At-home gold cleaning.

The simplest effective method: warm water, a drop of mild dish soap, soft toothbrush. Soak for 10–15 minutes. Gently brush grooves and crevices. Rinse under running water. Pat dry with a soft lint-free cloth. Air dry completely before storing.

  • Never use toothpaste — it is abrasive and scratches gold
  • Never use bleach or ammonia-based cleaners
  • For Kundan pieces, use a dry soft cloth only — no water
  • Store each piece separately in a soft pouch
  • Bring to us for free professional cleaning annually
