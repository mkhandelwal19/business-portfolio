The Process

From idea to your hands.

01

Consultation

Visit us at the store or connect on WhatsApp. Bring any reference — a photo, a sketch, a description. We'll discuss design, metal, stones, and budget. No fee for consultation.

02

Design Sketch

Our jewellery designer produces a hand sketch with dimensions and material specifications. Revisions are included until you're satisfied. No work begins until design is approved.

03

Crafting

Our master craftsman in Bow Bazaar makes the piece entirely by hand using traditional techniques. 3–6 weeks depending on complexity. Progress photos shared on WhatsApp.

04

Delivery

BIS hallmarking and final polishing before delivery. Piece arrives with certification, care instructions, and a valuation certificate on request. Free home delivery within Kolkata.

Heirloom Rework

Have an old piece? We can remake it.

Many clients bring in their mother's or grandmother's jewellery for reworking — either to resize, repair, or melt down and remake entirely into a new design while retaining the emotional significance of the original metal.

We also reset existing stones into new mounts. If you have diamonds or gemstones from an old piece, we can design an entirely new setting around them, giving the stones a second life.

  • Free assessment for all heirloom pieces
  • Old gold credit against new piece value
  • Existing stones re-set in new design
  • BIS hallmarking on all reworked pieces
