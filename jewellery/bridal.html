Bridal Consultation

Begin at least 3 months ahead.

A bridal jewellery consultation at Mallika Jewels is complimentary and unhurried. We discuss your wedding date, outfit, the ceremonies involved, and your budget. From there, we guide you through ready-to-wear and custom options.

Custom bridal sets require 6–10 weeks. Ready-to-wear sets are available for immediate purchase or reservation. On-site trial fittings available for all pieces.

  • Complimentary consultation — no obligation to purchase
  • On-site fittings for all bridal pieces
  • Pieces can be rented for photography sessions
  • Post-wedding cleaning and storage service
