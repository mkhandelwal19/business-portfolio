Our Guarantee

Every piece is certified.

All gold jewellery carries a BIS hallmark certifying purity. All diamonds are accompanied by an IGI certificate specifying cut, clarity, colour, and carat. We do not sell uncertified pieces, regardless of price point.

Older pieces can be brought in for re-hallmarking. We also offer independent diamond valuation certificates for insurance purposes.
