What We Sell

Craft categories, direct from source.

Handloom Textiles

Muslin from Murshidabad. Tant from Nadia. Jamdani from Fulia. Sourced directly from the weaving cooperatives. Each piece comes with weaver details.

Explore Textiles

Dhokra Metalwork

Lost-wax cast brass figurines and jewellery from Bankura and Bishnupur. No two pieces are identical. All items are made by individual artisan families.

Explore Metalwork

Kantha Embroidery

Hand-stitched Kantha quilts, stoles, and cushion covers. Each piece represents 40–80 hours of work by a single artisan woman from Bolpur or Birbhum.

Explore Kantha

Pottery & Terracotta

Bishnupur's terracotta work and Krishnanagar's legendary clay figurines. Decorative and functional pieces from West Bengal's pottery heritage.

Explore Pottery
