Our Story

Built for the craftspeople, not just the crafts.

Kolkata Craft Co. exists to put money in the hands of the people who make things.

Why We Started

The middleman was taking most of the margin.

Arjun Sen and Meghna Das spent three years working with NGOs in Birbhum and Bankura before starting Kolkata Craft Co. in 2019. What they consistently found: the craftspeople who spent 40 hours making a Kantha quilt were selling it to wholesale buyers for ₹800. It would reach a Delhi boutique at ₹6,000. The weaver got 13% of what the buyer paid.

Kolkata Craft Co. was built to invert that ratio. We buy directly from artisan families and cooperatives, price honestly, and publish the artisan margin on every product page. On average, the artisan receives 55–65% of what you pay — against an industry average of 10–20%.

How We Work

Six principles that shape every decision.

Direct Partnership

We work with 120+ artisan families and cooperatives directly. No aggregators, no middlemen. We visit each partner at least twice a year.

Transparent Pricing

Every product page shows the artisan's share. You know exactly how much of what you pay reaches the craftsperson.

Authenticity Certified

GI-tagged crafts come with official documentation. All pieces include a card with the artisan's name, village, and craft tradition.

Sustainable Materials

Natural fibres, natural dyes, traditional techniques. We don't stock products that use synthetic substitutes for traditional materials.

Free Shipping Over ₹999

All orders above ₹999 ship free across India. Fragile pottery and large items use specialised packaging designed at our end.

15-Day Returns

Honest return policy. If a piece arrives damaged or differs significantly from the listing, full refund. Artistic variation is part of handmade craft.
