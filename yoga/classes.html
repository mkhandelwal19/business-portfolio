Class Schedule

Find your practice.

Six class styles, morning and evening batches, seven days a week.

Hatha Yoga

Traditional postures with breath awareness. Suitable for beginners through intermediate practitioners. Focus on alignment and steadiness. 60 min · All levels.

Mon/Wed/Fri · 6:30 AM, 8:00 AM, 6:00 PM

Vinyasa Flow

Dynamic sequences linking breath to movement. More physically demanding. Builds strength, flexibility, and endurance. 60 min · Intermediate.

Tue/Thu/Sat · 7:00 AM, 7:30 PM

Yin Yoga

Long-hold passive postures that target connective tissue. Deeply restorative. Good for stress, tight hips, and nervous system recovery. 75 min · All levels.

Tue/Thu · 7:00 PM · Saturday: 8:30 AM

Pranayama & Meditation

Breath control and seated meditation practices. Ideal standalone or as supplement to asana practice. 45 min · All levels.

Daily · 5:30 AM · Sunday: 7:00 AM

Beginners' Foundations

8-week structured course for absolute beginners. Small groups (max 8). Covers postures, breath, and basic philosophy. 60 min · New to yoga.

Sat/Sun · 9:00 AM · New batch every 8 weeks

Pre/Postnatal Yoga

Adapted practice for pregnant women (trimester 2–3) and new mothers. Taught by certified prenatal instructor. Doctor clearance required. 60 min.

Wed/Sat · 10:00 AM · Max 6 per batch
