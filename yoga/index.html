What We Offer

Six paths to stillness

Classical disciplines taught with care. Every class rooted in tradition, adapted for the pace of contemporary life.

Hatha Yoga

The foundation of all yoga. Slow, deliberate postures that build strength, flexibility, and body awareness. Suitable for all ages and levels.

Vinyasa Flow

Breath-linked movement that builds heat and energy. Dynamic sequences for practitioners ready to deepen their practice.

Pranayama & Breathwork

Ancient breathing practices that calm the nervous system, improve lung capacity, and sharpen mental clarity.

Guided Meditation

Structured stillness. Guided sessions for beginners and experienced meditators to develop a consistent inner practice.

Kids Yoga

Playful, age-appropriate yoga for children aged 5–14. Builds focus, confidence, and physical coordination through movement and breath.

Corporate Wellness

Tailored programmes for workplace teams. Desk-friendly yoga, stress reduction, and breathwork for modern professionals.
