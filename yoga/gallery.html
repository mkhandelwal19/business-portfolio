The Studio

A space built for stillness.

Jute mats, natural light, wooden floors. No mirrors, no music, no distractions.

Main practice hall — 12 mats, jute floor
Dedicated meditation and pranayama room
East-facing windows — morning practice in natural light
Prop wall — quality blocks, straps, bolsters for all students
Studio overlooks a private garden — trees visible during practice
Reception with a small yoga philosophy library
Jute mats — natural, non-toxic, replaced every 6 months
Clean changing rooms with locker facilities
Rooftop terrace — weekend sunrise sessions by arrangement

Come see the studio.

Your first class is free. Walk-in visits during office hours welcome.
