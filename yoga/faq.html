Mirrors focus attention on external appearance — how you look in a pose — rather than how you feel in it. Classical yoga teaches internal awareness: the relationship between breath, sensation, and stillness. Mirrors undermine that. Most students find the adjustment difficult for the first week and transformative within the first month. If you genuinely cannot practice without them, Praana may not be the right fit — and we'd rather you know that upfront.
