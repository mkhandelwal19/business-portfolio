Our Teachers

Practitioners first, teachers second.

Three teachers. Combined 40 years of personal practice.

Kavita Nair
Founder & Lead Teacher
RYT-500 (Yoga Alliance). 15 years of practice, 8 years of teaching. Trained under T.K.V. Desikachar's lineage in Chennai and with senior teachers in Rishikesh. Founded Praana in 2018. Teaches Hatha, Pranayama, and private sessions. Studied Sanskrit and yoga philosophy formally for 3 years.
Hatha Pranayama Therapeutic Philosophy
Ananya Bose
Vinyasa & Prenatal Specialist
RYT-300. 10 years of practice, 5 years of teaching. Specialises in Vinyasa flow and prenatal yoga. Certified in prenatal and postnatal yoga through the Birthlight Foundation. Teaches the beginners' foundations course and all pre/postnatal classes. Background in physiotherapy (BPT, Calcutta University).
Vinyasa Prenatal Beginners Physiotherapy
Suresh Pillai
Yin & Meditation Teacher
E-RYT-200. 18 years of practice in Kerala Kalaripayattu tradition before transitioning to yoga and meditation. 6 years of teaching, focused on Yin and restorative practices. Studied Vipassana under the Goenka tradition. Teaches all evening Yin classes and the 5:30 AM pranayama sessions.
Yin Yoga Meditation Pranayama Restorative
