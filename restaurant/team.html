The People

Hands behind the plate.

The family and friends who make Aangan what it is — and have done so for decades.

Ratan Ghosh
Head Chef & Owner
Started washing dishes at 12. By 22, he was running the kitchen his grandfather built. Now carries four generations of recipe memory in his hands — and the quiet authority of someone who has never needed to raise his voice to get the stove to listen.
Priya Banerjee
Sous Chef
Trained in Lucknow at her nani's kitchen, came back to Kolkata and found her way to Aangan at 26. Specialises in the vegetarian board and the seasonal pickles that appear unannounced on the thali. Her Shukto is the reason many vegetarians become regulars.
Debashis Mondal
Manager
Eighteen years at Aangan. Knows every regular by name, their usual order, the table they prefer, and the fact that Mr. Sen always wants his dal a little on the thicker side. The restaurant's memory, wearing a kurta.
