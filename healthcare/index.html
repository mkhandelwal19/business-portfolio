What We Offer

Complete family care under one roof

From routine check-ups to specialist consultations — all your family's healthcare needs, handled with expertise and warmth.

General Medicine

Comprehensive care for adults — from acute infections and fever to long-term chronic disease management by Dr. Amitabha Basu.

Paediatric Care

Child health from infancy through adolescence. Vaccinations, growth monitoring, and illness care by Dr. Rohit Sharma, DCH.

Women's Health

Antenatal care, gynaecology consultations, Pap smear, menstrual health and menopause management by Dr. Sunita Basu, DGO.

Pathology Lab

In-house diagnostics including full blood panels, CBC, blood sugar, lipid profile — results same day or next morning.

Ultrasonography

On-site ultrasound scanning for abdomen, obstetrics and pelvis. Reports interpreted and explained immediately.

WhatsApp Consults

Follow-up consultations and prescription renewals over WhatsApp for existing patients. No commute required.
