Our Story

Started with a single room in Salt Lake.

Dr. Amitabha Basu opened the clinic in 1999 after a decade of hospital practice. He wanted a place where patients could be seen properly — not rushed, not reduced to a file number. A room, a desk, and the conviction that a 20-minute consultation is not a luxury.

By 2004, his wife Dr. Sunita Basu joined, bringing gynaecology and women's health. By 2012, the clinic had expanded to four consultation rooms, a pathology lab, and an ultrasonography unit. The Salt Lake neighbourhood grew; so did the families who walked through our doors.

Our Values

Medicine the way it should be.

Patient First

Every appointment is 15–20 minutes minimum. We don't rush. We don't guess. We examine properly and listen to what the patient says.

Complete Privacy

Patient records are confidential, stored securely, and never shared without consent. NABH-aligned data practices since 2010.

Honest Medicine

We prescribe what you need, not what generates a commission. If we can't help you, we refer you to someone who can — and follow up.

Family Care

Three generations of some families come to us. We know their histories, their habits, their concerns. That continuity is medicine too.

WhatsApp Follow-Up

After every visit, you can follow up via WhatsApp with questions about your prescription, test results or recovery. We respond the same day.

In-House Diagnostics

Pathology, ECG, and ultrasonography under one roof. Most results available same day. No unnecessary referrals to outside labs.
