Our Story

Started by a stylist, not a businessman.

Priya Mehta opened Lumière in 2014 after a decade working in Mumbai and Delhi salons. She returned to Kolkata with a clear vision: a salon that treats haircut appointments like consultation sessions, not conveyor belts.

What started as a 4-chair studio on Ballygunge Circular Road is now a 16-station salon and spa with a dedicated bridal suite, in-house nail studio, and a team of 12 trained specialists. Every technique is learnt before it's offered. Every product is patch-tested before it's stocked.

  • L'Oréal Professionnel & Schwarzkopf certified
  • All colourists trained in Paris-inspired techniques
  • VLCC and Kaya-aligned skin protocols
  • Bridal makeup artists with 200+ wedding portfolios
Our Philosophy

Beauty without the compromise.

Technique Over Trend

We don't chase Instagram fads. We master cuts and colour techniques that work for Indian hair texture, climate, and lifestyle — and then stay with them.

Products We Trust

Only L'Oréal Professionnel, Schwarzkopf Professional, and OLAPLEX on the floor. No parallel-import products. No undisclosed ingredients on sensitive scalps.

Time Respected

If your appointment is at 11 AM, your stylist is ready at 11 AM. We run a book, not a waiting room. Confirmed via WhatsApp 30 minutes before your slot.

Skin-Safe Standards

Patch tests for all colour treatments. Allergen-free nail polishes available. Dedicated kit for scalp-sensitive clients. We'd rather take 10 minutes extra than cause a reaction.

Bridal Excellence

Our bridal team has dressed over 200 brides since 2016. Every bridal booking includes a mandatory trial session and skin prep regime starting 6 weeks before the date.

Privacy & Comfort

Dedicated bridal suite. Private skin consultation rooms. Women-only spa area. A space where you can relax without an audience — or the pressure to buy a package.
