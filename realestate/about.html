Our Story

Started with one rule: never show a property you wouldn't live in.

Pranab Saha started the firm in 1998 from a rented office on Park Street with two desks and a telephone. In an industry built on commissions and quick closes, he had a different model: take the time to understand what the client actually needs, and only then show them properties.

It took longer. Deals fell through. Other brokers moved faster. But Pranab's clients kept coming back — and sending referrals — because they trusted what they were told. By 2005, word of mouth alone had made Saha Properties one of the three most recognised names in Kolkata residential property.

His daughter Riya joined in 2015, bringing a corporate background and digital systems to a business that had run entirely on relationships and paper. The combination works: the same integrity, significantly more information, and faster execution than the firm had in its early years.

How We Work

Six commitments to every client.

Needs Assessment First

Before we show a single property, we spend time understanding your actual requirements — family size, commute, budget, timeline, and what you're compromising on versus what's non-negotiable.

Full Legal Verification

Every property is checked for clear title, approved plans, RERA registration, and encumbrance status before we show it to you. We do not list properties with title disputes.

Transparent Fees

Our brokerage is 1% of the transaction value, charged to both buyer and seller separately. No hidden fees, no inflated developer commissions that drive us to push specific projects.

Loan Assistance

We work with SBI, HDFC, ICICI, Axis, and LIC Housing Finance. We negotiate on your behalf and often secure rates 0.15–0.25% better than standard walk-in offers.

End-to-End Documentation

Registration, mutation, property tax transfer, society NOC — we handle all post-sale paperwork. Our job is done when you have the keys and the documents, not when the sale closes.

After-Sale Support

Questions about the property after purchase? Construction defects, neighbour disputes, maintenance society issues — we remain available. Our relationship is not transactional.
