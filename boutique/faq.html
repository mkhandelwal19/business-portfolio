FAQ

Everything you need to know.

From sizing to bespoke to bridal — answered honestly.

We stock sizes 32–52 in most ready-to-wear styles, and 32–56 for festive and occasion wear. All our display pieces are available across the full size range — we don't stock token sizes. Bespoke orders are made to your exact measurements with no upper limit on size.

You select the fabric and style, we take your measurements in-store, and the garment is made by our tailoring unit in 10–14 business days. There are two fitting sessions included: an intermediate fitting at 7 days and a final fitting before collection. Bespoke orders require a 50% advance. The balance is paid on collection.

Yes, via Blue Dart and Delhivery for ready-to-wear. Delivery takes 3–5 business days to most Indian cities. Bespoke and bridal orders are not shipped — they require in-store fitting sessions. Shipping is free on orders above ₹5,000. Returns for online orders are accepted within 7 days if the item is unworn and has tags.

In-store: exchanges within 14 days with receipt, on unworn items with original tags. No cash refunds — store credit only. Online: returns accepted within 7 days of delivery. Bespoke and sale items are non-returnable. We always encourage trying items in-store before purchase to avoid this situation entirely.

For a bespoke bridal lehenga or saree, 3–4 months is ideal. Ready-to-wear bridal pieces from our collection can be altered and ready within 3–4 weeks. November–February is peak bridal season — we are typically booked out for bespoke bridal from September onwards. Contact us as early as possible.

We source from handloom cooperatives and certified weaving clusters across India: Chanderi silk from Madhya Pradesh, Banarasi from Varanasi, handloom cotton from Shantiniketan, and block-printed fabrics from Jaipur and Barmer. Natural dye fabrics use indigo, turmeric, and madder sourced from organic farms. We can trace every fabric to its source and are happy to share that information.

Yes. We accept customer-supplied fabric for tailoring. Standard tailoring charges apply based on the garment type and complexity. Please bring the fabric in-store and we'll assess it for suitability. We work with cotton, silk, georgette, chiffon, and most natural-fibre blends. Heavily beaded or pre-embellished fabric may attract an additional charge.

Yes, basic alterations (length, waist, blouse fitting) are complimentary for purchases above ₹3,000 within 30 days. Complex alterations (restructuring, adding fabric, changing silhouette) are charged based on the work involved. Our in-store tailor is available Tuesday to Saturday.
