Our Story

Fashion rooted in craft.

Kolkata-born. Artisan-made. Designed for women who know what they want.

The Beginning

Built on a love of handcraft and real fit.

Nandini Ghosh opened Riyaaz in 2017 after 8 years as a buyer for export garment houses in Kolkata. She'd spent nearly a decade knowing what Indian craft was capable of — and watching it get exported abroad while the local market got fast fashion. Riyaaz was the correction.

The name comes from the Urdu word for practice — the daily ritual of refining a skill. Every piece at Riyaaz is made with artisans from West Bengal and Odisha, using block printing, kantha stitch, zardozi embroidery, and natural dye techniques passed down through generations.

  • Artisans from Murshidabad, Shantiniketan & Pipili
  • All fabrics sourced from Indian mills and handloom cooperatives
  • Bespoke tailoring in 10–14 days
  • Sizes 32–52 in most styles — real sizing for real women
Our Values

Fashion that means something.

Artisan-Made

Every piece is made by craftspeople, not machines. We work directly with weaver collectives and embroidery artisans — no middlemen, fair wages, and traceability to the creator.

Real Sizing

We stock sizes 32–52 in most styles, and offer alterations on everything. Clothes should fit the body, not the other way around. Our fitting room has a tailor, not just a mirror.

Conscious Sourcing

Chanderi from Madhya Pradesh. Handloom cotton from Shantiniketan. Natural indigo from Bengal. We know where every fabric comes from and we're happy to tell you.

Bespoke Service

Bring your vision, your occasion, your measurements. We'll build it. Bespoke orders take 10–14 days and include two fitting sessions. Available for bridal and non-bridal alike.

Seasonal Collections

Four collections per year, plus festive drops for Durga Puja, Eid, and winter weddings. Each collection is limited-run — when it's gone, it's gone. No overproduction.

WhatsApp Styling

Share your occasion and body type — we'll curate three options for you before you even walk in. Styling advice is free for all customers, in-store or via WhatsApp.
