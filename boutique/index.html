What we offer

Crafted for the contemporary Indian wardrobe

From everyday handloom to once-in-a-lifetime trousseau — every piece leaves our studio with a weaver's name behind it.

Handloom Sarees

Taant, Jamdani, Baluchari and Kantha — each saree woven by master craftspeople in Bengal's weaving villages.

Contemporary Kurtas

Handloom cotton and silk kurta sets designed in-house — modern cuts that respect the fabric's natural character.

Artisan Dupattas

Lightweight Jamdani and Kantha dupattas — the perfect accent to any outfit, handwoven to drape beautifully.

Custom Tailoring

Choose your fabric, share your measurements — our in-house tailor delivers a garment made exactly for you.

Festive Edit

A curated seasonal collection for Durga Puja, Diwali and weddings — statement pieces that photograph beautifully.

WhatsApp Styling

Share your occasion and body type — our stylist sends you personalised fabric and draping suggestions within 2 hours.
